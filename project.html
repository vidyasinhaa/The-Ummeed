BHOOKH

Welcome to The Ummeed Foundation, where we are committed to supporting Sustainable Development Goal 2: Zero Hunger through innovative solutions.We are driven by the belief that food is a basic human right, and through collective effort, we can make a tangible difference in our communities.Our platform harnesses the power of community and technology to bridge the gap between surplus food and
At this project, we partner with local restaurants to minimize food waste and maximize impact. Through the installation of advanced thermal steel containers, equipped with separate compartments for rice, bread, curry, vegetarian dishes, and non-vegetarian options, we ensure that donated food remains fresh and organized. These containers are designed with special features to preserve warmth and freshness, guaranteeing that the meals provided to those in need are not only nutritious but also satisfying.
When these containers are filled with surplus or donated food, our system automatically notifies our dedicated volunteers via our website. They swiftly collect the donations from the restaurants and distribute them to local shelters, food banks, and directly to individuals facing food insecurity.
Join us in our mission to create a sustainable and compassionate future where every meal counts. Together, we can make a significant impact in our community and beyond, ensuring that no one goes hungry while promoting responsible food consumption.
