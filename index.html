The Ummeed Foundation

The Ummeed Foundation is a not-for-profit organisation headquartered in
Ranchi, India. The Foundation strives to eliminate classroom hunger by the donetaion funds . It provides nutritious
meals to children,poors,beggers and the neddy.
Ummeed also aims to counter malnutrition and support the Right
to Education of children hailing from socio-economically challenging
backgrounds.
